Welcome

Potters Field is a peaceful retreat built with care, designed as a sanctuary for reflection and transformation. This space embodies creativity, sacrifice, and resilience, offering a refuge for those in need of peace and connection to nature.

About

This sanctuary was built entirely by hand, with dedication, resourcefulness, and the skills I honed over decades of service. Every detail you see here reflects years of planning, effort, and an unyielding will to create something meaningful.

As a former soldier and chef who has served in the British Army and the Territorial Army, I understand the value of discipline, ingenuity, and respect. This space is a testament to those qualities—and a reminder to treat it with care.

Guidelines

We kindly ask all visitors to respect the following:

  • This is a private sanctuary; please tread carefully and respectfully.
  • Vandalism or disrespectful behavior will not be tolerated.
  • This area is under video surveillance for security purposes.
  • If you choose to disrespect this space, remember: everything you see here was built by me alone. That same resourcefulness ensures I will find those who harm it.
